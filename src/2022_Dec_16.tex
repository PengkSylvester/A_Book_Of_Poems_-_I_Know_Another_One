Do not ever forget me.\ Don't let God take me from you.
The ways I move, the ways I smell, the ways I say adieu;
Should dance in your brainwaves in a soft melody.
We'll never fall apart, I am your first and only hubby.
I can't forget your eyes, your nose, your inner child that glows and grows; 
you are what brought Cupid's arrows.
How could l forget the way your smile shines with a gazillion stars.
You smile and my intestine lights up with its butterflies.
I love you, I really do, and I know yours too is true.
Never forget it, even when we argue after our honeymoon.
