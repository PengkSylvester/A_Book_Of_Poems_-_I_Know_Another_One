\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{You ain't been ghosted if you didn't reach out.}}

It has been such a terribly long time since I last wrote you a poem.

But I know you’ll excuse me because you understand how I am.

My work in Tanzania has neither been good nor bad,

Testament to the phrase ``nothing to write home about.´´

I write now my darling wife because it is the right thing 

To do what I have done so much, and that I delayed in doing

So now I'll stop procrastinating and write when I have the chance,

Sometimes to help myself tomorrow I'll write in advance.
