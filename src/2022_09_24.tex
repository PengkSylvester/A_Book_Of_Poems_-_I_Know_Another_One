\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{You can't go back and change the beginning, but you can start where you are and change the ending.}}

We can't ever go back home.\ It has changed.\ It has evolved.

Every meeting is about creating war or trying to return from war.

A backpack is for the former, a backed baby for the latter.

Love is for the former, heartbreak happens in the latter

A marriage is for the former, a family home is for the latter.

Do both together, mother and father.

Hard times create strong men.\ Strong men create good times.

Good times create weak men.\ Weak men create hard times.

History repeats itself in these patterns; it rhymes.
