\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{This was inspired by Fragile Dogubo`s piece titled `Breakfast'.}}

This poem is about breakfast, but not the British kind.

His moniker is Burna, he prefers his meals African.
%His moniker's Oluwa Burna, he prefers his meals African.

%His heartbreaks are served in generous Ewa-laden pans.
His heartbreaks are served in generous tripe-laden pans.

%The daughters of the soil wash their hands and sit
The daughters of the soil sit down and wash their hands

before dipping ring-less fingers into his Yoruba dish.

%He tinders for the next girl, while the fed girl's still in bed.
He swipes for the next girl, while a fed girl's still in bed.

But when will they know he cares nothing for their Toastings.

And he prefers to focus on the Bread instead.
