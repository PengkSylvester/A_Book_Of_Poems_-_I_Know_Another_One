As you go about life you must suffer some pain.

Here's a virtue that averts you from going insane.

Feelings are all things neither good nor bad for you.

Catch yourself feel the feelings felt true and through.

Throw a tantrum, or respond ``oh look, something new''.

Your body now wants air, food, or whatever it wants to do.

Take some time to breathe or pray on this new feeling this new day. 

Patience is a virtue not to be thrown away.
