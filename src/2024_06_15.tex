I don't have any secrets, as long as Marvelene asks to hear,
but I do have some information that I'm not going to volunteer, 
and that she wouldn't ask from me, just out of the blue.
So, here is something that I'll tell to you. And it's true.
I have had a death wish ever since I was twelve years old,
to be blessed enough to glimpse what the afterlife holds.
I've begged for my entropic self-control to be taken away,
To stop the burden of performance, so I may simply spectate.
