\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{Some of us go poor in attempts to appear rich.}}


People's choices seem to be motivated by either desire or fear

That’s one of the most fundamental truths of human behavior

Desire drives us to gain touches, wealth, respect, or success

Fear drives us to avoid; pain, loss, shame, failure, or regrets

Every choice can be balanced between what we want and what we dread

But here’s the twist; people often believe they’re acting with a logical head

I could think I desire my choice, when fear is what’s really steering me

Like the need to buy a nicer house because I fear living in poverty
