Create in me a pure heart, O God,
And a steadfast spirit that gives a shit.
Let me be contented to be bored,
And have no interest in being a bitch.
A heart free from sin, guilt, corruption,
Capable of sincere devotion to You.
Please take away all of my temptations
That are indeed unwise and irresistible
