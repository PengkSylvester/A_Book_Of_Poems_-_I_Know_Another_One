\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{It is urgent to live an enchanted life.} \newthought{- Valter Hugo Mãe}}

I adore you, My Queen Marvelene.

And I don’t write it only because you call me King

I know how much you like to hear your title.

I write it to tell you I love you. I will always love you.

I want to comfort and take care of you. As you do too.

I want to have dilemmas to discuss with you,

To give you little easy peasy social projects for you to do.

And I'd love it for you to give me chores or tasks too. 

Open jars, mowing yards, driving cars, body-guard what's ours, you can choose.

You are so much better than anyone else alive.

I love you in so many ways so much I can't descrive.

I can’t help it, darling, for I have met many girls, 

But within two convos they all seem like ashen shells. 

You are the only one I want. And I know you want me too.

My Queen, I do adore you.
