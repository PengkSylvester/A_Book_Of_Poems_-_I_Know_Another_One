\textit{[MARVELENE]}
Could you write a poem for me that revolves around advice?
Like yesterday's own, it could all end in similar lines
You mention a situation, and say “It's not a big deal”
Let me give you a couple of examples to get what I mean;
‘Don't ignore, nor shout over broken glass just like a toddler.
Be productive, tidy up the shards.\ It's not a big deal’, or
‘Why is it mandatory in some parts for women to birth a male?
Simply change one daughter's gender.\ It's not a big deal.’
