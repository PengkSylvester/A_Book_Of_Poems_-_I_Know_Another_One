\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{Divide each difficulty into as many parts as is feasible and necessary to resolve it.} \newthought{- Descartes}}

I argue with my brain, I investigate my own thoughts
Like an enemy trying to pick apart my arguments

I write down things I don't agree with yet, then debate them to myself
And in so doing, I kill the weak ones, and only keep the apex

Scientist do this same thing; look for blind-spots to their theories
At least the good ones do; try to delete their deformed babies

Because who needs ideas that can't stand the test of time
`Survival of the fittest', says my mentor Charles Darwin
