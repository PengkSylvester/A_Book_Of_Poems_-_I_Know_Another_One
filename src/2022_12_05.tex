It is an uncomfortable item since my neck has some girth.
It is not very effective for a tourniquet, like a belt.
If I ever need to console someone in grief,
the necktie can't wipe my tears like a handkerchief.
I will admit it saves a shirt from drips and dribble
But we don't need a bib or, napkin always, it's simple.
In these closing clothing statements, I hope you realise
that the revolution will not be televised.
