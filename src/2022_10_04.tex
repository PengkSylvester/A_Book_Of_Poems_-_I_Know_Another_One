I want to grow up to be a clean gentleman.

Physically and spiritually spic and span.

It may be hard to attain it, but I just won't quit.

Be an elder with an ember of wisdom and wit.

It won't be so difficult, if I come to think of it.

Cause there's so much filth around; so much smut abound.

Our amount of crowned clowns is soundly astounding.

Forget them, eat from my spoon without wiping.
