I was a twelve-year-old when I watched my first beheading.
It was a 20-minute video, message sent to my BlackBerry.
Picture the light from the screen reflecting on my boyish face,
As I listened to the screams of ``Allahu-Akbar'' all over the place.
There was so much blood spilled, and the victims didn't protest.
I forget if they put one's cut head on his or her back or chest.
And now that I've grown up, to work with Muslims at my job,
What do I do with all that trauma?\ I write this certified bop.
