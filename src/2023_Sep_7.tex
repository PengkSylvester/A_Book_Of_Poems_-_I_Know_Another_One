\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{It is true that so far as wealth gives time for ideal ends, and exercise to ideal energies, wealth is better than poverty and ought to be chosen. But those who persist in believing wealth brings happiness are to be pitied.}}

In a way, it was my patient joy of the process that made me write everyday.

And as I practiced, it got easier, the man in the mirror was made of clay.

I may say, the greats who I listened to, were who moulded my play,

Lil Dicky, Kanye - from the US. Shuffle-T, Marlo - from the UK.

From Puerto Rico - Lin-Manuel. From Nigeria - Havfy, Adichie.

That was my bouquet of non-clichés that made a good gourmet in any soirée.

Those were the writers that inspired me to fulfill my calling.

In leaps and bounds, my amount of crowned psalms was soundly outstanding.
