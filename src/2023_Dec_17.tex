I love to hear the magic words ‘Welcome Back’,
Even in the places I haven't been in the past.
It implies a sequel, a reconnection to number one,
An adventure, an undertaking of a new war
Whether it's your first time visiting Tokyo, Japan or
Your second tenure as President Barack Obama,
A ‘Welcome Back’ with your name, and a smile
Could put you fucking floating on cloud nine.
