\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{Insight.}}

The first person to cry in my arms was my little brother.
He was just a child, angry and disappointed at his drunk mother.
I was a guest. He felt shame. He felt self-conscious.
I held him and I felt helpless. The boy's only solace.
The second person to ever cry in my arms was Miss Fricker.
She got a chance to confess some of her unresolved trauma.
I held her I felt powerless. And after some silence,
She laughed a laugh - while crying - straight into my face.
This pain I feel from these memories, when will it end?
The third person ever, happened yesterday and was with my girlfriend.
