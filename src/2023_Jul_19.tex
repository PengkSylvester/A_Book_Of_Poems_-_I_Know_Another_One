The first person to cry in my arms was my little step-brother.
He was just a child, angry and disappointed at his drunk mother.
I was their guest. He felt ashamed. He felt self-conscious.
I held him and I felt helpless. My boy's only solace.
The second person to ever cry in my arms was Miss Fricker.
She got a chance to confess some of her unresolved trauma.
I held her and I felt helpless. And after some silence,
She laughed a laugh - while crying - that burned into my iris.
This pain I feel from these memories, when will it end?
The third person ever, cried yesterday and it was my girlfriend.
