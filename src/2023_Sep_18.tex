We went ahead with our marriage ceremony, and lived happily ever after.

We didn't publish my anthology because the tell-all would bring disaster.

Marvelene kept male friends; offers of sex were where any peace talks ceased.

I kept female friends; women who were family, professionals, or deceased.

My story is not a recommendation, you and I are different people, don't forget.

This was how `I' wooed your mother, with very many poetry, and killing a lot.

I just hope to have given you a thing or two to learn in this \textit{danse macabre},

Just don't publish anything years later in a poem to confess to murder.
