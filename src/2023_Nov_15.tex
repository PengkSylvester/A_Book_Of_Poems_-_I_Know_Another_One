We can make change.\ We can make change.
Bring forward your money and we can exchange.
We've got pence and sense that we shall never lack.
We've got more quarters than a police barrack.
We've got dimes, two-penny, and nickels,
We can make change, complex or simples.
We've got fifty pence, your fiddy cents' brother.
We've got all the nation's denominations for whatever.
