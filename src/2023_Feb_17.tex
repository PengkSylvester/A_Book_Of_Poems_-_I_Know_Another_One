\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{He who has a house of glass 
should beware of hostile stones that pass,
and much least should he cast!}}

Two days ago, I found out that Annabelle just got married.
So, by now, Joe Taylor has disvirgined my crush post-marriage
She's now a wife.\ What a life.\ And soon to be a parent.
It's a logical progression from their dating, and meeting parents.
I'm not perturbed at them; they upped and then went on without me.
But suddenly, I am jealous; writing poems is not ``easy-peasy''\ldots
Life is a series of reactions to environment and culture,
and for not possessing or receiving her love, here's my feedback.
I just pray, for Annabelle's sake, that they are both soulmates. 
My life moves along away from Annabelle, my only heartbreak.
