I choose to lose myself so deeply while in theatre,
That it doesn't even register that I'm in a drama.
It is real life, as far as my feelings are concerned.
I am engaging with a reality whenever I perform.
Of course, I make myself forget the things I know will happen next,
And just focus on reacting to the actors on the set.
It takes a while to get myself into the right perfect headspace,
After some trial, it clicks in place; speak natural lies from my face.
