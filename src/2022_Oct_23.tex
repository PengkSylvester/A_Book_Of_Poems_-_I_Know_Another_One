\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{What is your excuse for not being all you know you could be? Do you say ``I don't have a person to help me''? Yes you do, you have Jesus in front of you.} \newthought{- Sunday Sermon}}

Babyboy, ``Where's my perfect partner?'' is the wrong question,
and ``Who can I mould into perfection?'' could also cause depression.
Mould yourself - ask more, not less, of the man in the mirror.
Become the perfect date myself, instead of searching for her prior.
Or else my perfect date won't like me - won't choose me out of the many.
I'll be caught if I'm a catch, the ocean has fishes aplenty.
If my country sucks, what grade of citizen am I to it?
If my dealt cards suck, what grade of player am I to it?
