\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{Happiness lies in thinking or doing that which one considers beautiful.} \newthought{- Hazrat Inayat Khan}}


The older I get, the less I want to be impressive.

Just quiet wealth that doesn't need to explain itself.

I want mornings that don't rush me.

Afternoons that don't drain me.

And nights with no homework,

Cuddling, watching a movie.

The chances to choose exercise, to play and breathe.

When it stops being fun, that's my cue to leave.
