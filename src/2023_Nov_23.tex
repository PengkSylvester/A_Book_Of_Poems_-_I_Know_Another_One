If you are given five loaves of bread, and two small fishes,
To share between five thousand men, with children and misses,
Don't share it on a `first-to-come, first-to-be-served' basis,
Nor sell it to a high bidder, nor wait for more to be served first.
It would also be irresponsible to try sharing out tiny morsels,
So, that was \textbf{not} what Jesus did on the mount, silly mortals.
Instead, close your eyes and think; Who here \textbf{needs} these portions?
Therefore the food goes firstly to those without private sources.
