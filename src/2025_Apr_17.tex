What if I gave you two hundred million,

Where would you put it?\ What’s your reason?

And not in your Nigerian Naira,

Instead, in dollars\ldots But Canadian.

After some of the definite wastage,

Such as a mansion in your village—

Would you go deep into \textit{real} real estate?

Now that you finally have the leverage?

Surely, that would reduce people's suffering,

Since your service would be their blessing.

So now that you understand investing,

What would you do with two hundred thousand?
