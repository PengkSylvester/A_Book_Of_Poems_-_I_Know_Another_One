\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{We live, knowing we will die, but not knowing how or when; we have children, knowing they too will die, but not how or when; we entertain a story, knowing all stories end, but not knowing how or when—all in the hope that in the end, it's beautiful.}}


\textit{Pengk does not intend to be treated as a gorgeous specimen.
Pengk expects to die ultimately as many other great men. 
But the difference is this; He is Pengk, he'll be missed.
Because no else can give to the world so many gifts.
You'd be hard-pressed to put in Pengk's daily acts of service.
Pengk uses words of affirmations like a social genius,
and his hugs — his neck hugs! — give me butterflies, like drugs.
I like the way he walks, fucks, talks, listens, and loves.}
