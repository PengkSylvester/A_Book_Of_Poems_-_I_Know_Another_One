\textit{[TO MY FATHER]}
\vspace{0.4cm}

Onowu sir, I pray you’re having a good day.
\vspace{0.4cm}
Well, yesterday took an embarrassing unexpected turn of events.
\vspace{0.4cm}
It was quite unfortunate that I missed my 10:35 a.m.\ flight.\ I didn’t treat the departure time with as much respect as it
should have gotten.\ I arrived at the counter 30 minutes before the flight time, and was told that they had submitted their
passenger manifest to the airplane, and there was nothing they could do.
\vspace{0.4cm}
It had felt like an out-of-body surreal experience because I always heard about it happening to other people but I had
never thought of it happening to me, and until yesterday it had indeed never happened to me.
\vspace{0.4cm}
My only viable option was to pay ₦10,000 to reschedule the flight for this morning instead.\ I had to remain in Abuja
despite expecting to be in Asaba by yesterday.
\vspace{0.4cm}
I am still very energised for today as I was for yesterday, and I’m now standing in front of the airport terminal at 8:08
a.m., ready to make today count.
\vspace{0.4cm}
Barring any unforeseen circumstances, I can confirm that I’ll be in your city today, sir.\ I’ll call you when I board the
airplane.
\vspace{0.4cm}
Here’s a Good Morning wish sir from
\vspace{0.4cm}
Your dutiful humble Son.

\end{fullwidth}
