My dearest, Maria, how long more till you are really here?
My wife is thinking to wait until the coast is fully clear.

How do I convince her to create you and give you to me?
I want to be responsible for my baby in my custody.

You'll grow by my side as I take you everywhere I go,
Be it the bank, the mines, or even in meetings with the gov.

I want you there for all of it; like a daddy-daughter duo.
I'll feed you, clothe you, and we'll be each other's shadow.

I'll see all your behaviors, and you'll see how I treat people,
I don't want to be in anywhere that won't accept you.

Let's move through the world together, hand-in-hand, handling
Whatever we get; sorrow and joy and everything in between.
