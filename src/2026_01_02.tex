\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{The poem is to be read in a transylvanian hungarian accent.}}

You have spent hundreds of thousands of dollars for the project,

Only for it to have been improperly constructed by a non-expert.

The person you hired as your contractor has royally fucked you,

And I have sadly tagged along to learn from your misfortune.

Everything that is ugly, cruel, stupid, but most of all, ugly

Is his fault.\ You gave him the cross to bear and all authority.

I have learnt what to do for my own future infrastructures,

And I am sorry to leave you now, but I must do what is best for us.
