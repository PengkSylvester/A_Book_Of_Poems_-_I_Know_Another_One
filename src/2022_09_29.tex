This poem is about competition, the drive to surpass

Without competition, everyone carries last

`Surpass' and `last' was a bad rhyme, but I did it

My effort for rhyming is waning and I can feel it.

Must I even rhyme?\ Must I even make substantial sense?

I'm not getting paid to write, I'm making zero cents.

and where is my competent rival that I must outplay?
