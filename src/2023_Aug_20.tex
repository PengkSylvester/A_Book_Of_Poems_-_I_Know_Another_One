\textit{Mother, I have gone out and found myself a brother
A man I want related to you in one way or the other
His praise and worship of me is for proving his love without death
He martyrs, teary-eyed and singing, because his love is great
I love him as a brother, and I have faith you'll love him as a son
And I know, according to God's design, that he'll love you as a mom
Because you helped to create the love of his life, Miss Osha
King Pengk, your \textbf{Weng}, is not my `close friend', he is my brother.}
