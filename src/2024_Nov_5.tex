I wish I could just plug your brain into mine,
To show you all the beautiful things I've seen.
If you went behind my eyes, here's what you'd find.
A gorgeous goddess child named Marvelene.
It's just that, if \textit{you} looked into my mind,
You'd surely see something else that's obscene.
I've killed to survive in times I'd have surely died.
Don't mind my long life full of conquer and shame.
