\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{Strive to be internally driven. Driven to compete with yourself, not with other people. If you compete with other people, you end up in this mimetic trap, and you sort of play this tournament. Even if you ‘win’, you lose. But if you’re competing with yourself, and all you’re trying to do is be the best possible version you can—there’s no limit to how far that can drive you to perform.} \newthought{- Sam Altman
}}

Are you on a local flight, or International?
Does your inner ear hurt you right now?
If yes; it helps to depressurize your ear
By holding shut your nose, and pushing air
Gently towards your nose that's held shut,
Until you hear your inner ear crackle and pop.
You can stop pushing the air then, and settle.
Your ears have depressurised to a new level.
