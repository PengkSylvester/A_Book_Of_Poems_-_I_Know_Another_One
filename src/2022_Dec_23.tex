Who dares face me?\ I erase thee,
quick on my precise killing spree.
You fight - a sad dismal chore,
As I grin at your tears, blood and gore.
Your strength and will, I take as mine, 
You, a distant whisper in time.
To face me next, don't be so fast, 
lest your mortal coil is free at last.
