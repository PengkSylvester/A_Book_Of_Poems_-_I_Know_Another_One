Sometimes I feel like a motherless child a long way from home.
The straying could be worse, of course, but I wish I hadn't gone
Off course, in this desolate land of strangers with dangerous faces.
Where am I? Who am I? How long have I been in these places?
If I could just turn back time, all this would be different.
I am desperate, my dear friends, to the unsealing of my fate,
%Our fates!\ Dear God, please let me out of this stalemate.
As I turn from unfocused ways, to look to your wise precepts.
Forgive my sins, heal my face, ameliorate this hand I'm dealt.
