\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{I can't ward off fear. But I bravely step into my arena despite it; to know the great enthusiasms that come in victory, and the great devotions that come in defeat, from the pursuit of a worthy cause.}}

I used to see life's rhymes, but not anymore 
I no longer notice interesting patterns, I'm bored
I'm on the floor, looking up, thinking ``this is overwork, for sure''
I've grown complacent, perfunctory, I could go on and on
My best luck is I am prepared for this like a good boy scout
My best work should not include despair or burn-out
The future won't build itself though, I'll water it to sprout
This book is all about discipline.\ You try seeding in a drought
