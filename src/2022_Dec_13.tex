\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{The only time you peer in your peer's bowl is to make sure that they have enough.}}

Dear sir, I hope this letter finds you feeling supreme.
I seek to acquaint you of my affection and esteem for Marvelene.
Your daughter, I would not, sir, have bad intentions for.
She's dutifully yours and, in me, she sees your honour.
If I may prove myself to you, worthy of that honour, I pray,
with your blessing, to make her happy consistently.
Sir, I brazenly request the favour of an evening conversation,
to fully explain myself to your positive satisfaction.
Until then, I remain Yours faithfully, sir
Signed; \textit{Engr.~Pengk Sylvester}
