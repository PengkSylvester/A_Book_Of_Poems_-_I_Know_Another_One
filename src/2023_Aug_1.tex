It's about time I talked about Kokoye's death
The man was an army sargent that I respected
He seemed immortal, full of energy, with a badass haircut
I felt alive around him, he was the life of the campus
I didn't get a chance with sarge to chant one last hymn
He died not according to the charts I had planned for him
Before he went inanimated, terminated, uncompensated by me
I'll be damned if I didn't pay him back by telling his story.
