On the night of Thirty-first December, Twenty-Nineteen,
I smoked marijuana with Hubert and had a bad feeling.
The guy had a large tolerance for weed, but I did not.
I felt distraught and knew it was because of the pot.
Death, or betrayal, or brain-rot didn't matter to me anymore,
I kept my cool as the kush carried me. I was fine by the first firework.
I survived the train trip and remained high for three days.
I have now learned. I never want to try another hit of MJ
