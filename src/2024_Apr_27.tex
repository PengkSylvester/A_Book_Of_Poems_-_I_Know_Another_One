There are some poems that I don't remember writing,
And some aspects that I don't see at first glance,
It tends to capture what I was thinking and feeling,
Even though it's riddled with room for improvements.
But I keep it as it is, because it'd felt right on that day,
I try to protect the Me that wrote it, and not edit what I see,
Because whether or not I find impressive what he had to say,
It helps to stay on nodding terms with the person I used to be.
