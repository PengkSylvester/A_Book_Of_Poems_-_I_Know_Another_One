I miss you, dear compass, it has been too long
Since I last consulted your counsel - that's wrong.
Please show me True North again, how far I've strayed,
I need you by my side to chart our voyage.
Please ground me, don't leave me to be lost at sea.
I'm too scared of being adrift and lonely,
Like a year ago, when Hiba left me to myself.
I need you, dear compass, I have no one else.
