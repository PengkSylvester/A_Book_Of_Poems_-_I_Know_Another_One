\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{Write a Fucking Memo.} \newthought{- Sharran Srivatsaa}}

First give the background, the context, the ``The story so far''
Then the ``Based on this, here's the issue that I want to solve''

While they're thinking, pondering how you'll get that done
Then you hit 'em with the ``Here's my recommendation''

Now they know what you want, why it's wanted, and how you might get it
So you propose the parlay, the discussion, the ``Where and when can we meet?''

Remember, there can be no agreements made without communications
Silence can get you nothing, invitation alone gets answers to questions
