When no one else would do it, you did what it took.
I was starving for attention, and only you let me cook.
And I don't mean with a hot cast-iron pan for a great sear,
A brown crust on all six sides touched with salt and pepper,
Then reduce the heat to medium and add 2 tbs of butter,
Spoon the sauce over the steak for now, and add lemon juice after.
You let me, nay, you made me to write and to speak freely,
Holding my hand along the way as I cooked up these me-mes.
That's the kind of cooking I mean; a combination of ideas.
You made your nigga find his dish, and that shit gives me happy tears.
