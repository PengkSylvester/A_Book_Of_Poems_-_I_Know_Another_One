What should I do if someone throws a rock at my head
With enough force to harm me, but not to leave me dead
All without provocation; I didn't insult him nor scuff his shoes
He bruised my ego, my pride, as stupidly as Stupid does
To stomp his chest to his death, would be undefendable in court
The general principle is to shoot him, but I don't have a gun
This revenge has to be done by someone else, for my behalf
My friend can beat him up, OR the court gives me all his dollars
As long as someone else harvests an eye for the eye that I lost
Then I can forgive the despicable perpetrator of the assault
