Only to see someone loosen its thread to unravel it, my godmother


The next time I came, she had knitted the thread to a blanket my size


Their way of seeing the world was fundamentally different in my eyes


Everything around them was something they could see as something new


Where I saw a sweater, it was a thread shaped as one from her point of view


She saw more degrees of freedom than I did, and acted on it
