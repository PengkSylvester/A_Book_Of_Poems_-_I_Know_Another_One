It's a good day to fly in the air, defying gravity
Way back B.C.\ doing this would have killed me.
I face the runway, and deftly accelerate
Till the wind rushes under me to make me levitate.
Slowly, I rise towards the cloud in the skies,
its cotton-candiness can't stop me, but it tries,
My body glides right past the first Strato Cumulus,
I look down and see the glorious tininess of us.
It looks familiar to me, it's the reason I have a death wish.
But my final destination isn't to be a today's crash.
