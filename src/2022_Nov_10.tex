\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{The true value of what I do is not how much it is worth to me, but how much it benefits you.} \newthought{- Arthur C. Brooks}}

The most important decisions in one's life are hard to teach. 
Like, one has to figure out for oneself who to work for or work with. 
Figure out how to get along, how to listen, how to provoke. 
Who to marry, where to live, how to recognise luck. 
How to deal, how to be real, how to balance one's ego. 
Like, I recognise that I might be unique but not special. 
How to be curious, how to be scared, how to expect and to react. 
Figure it out with courage, justice, wisdom, self-control, and all that.
