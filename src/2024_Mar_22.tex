The doorway to time is a two-sided portal.
So, unless an older version of your physical
Body comes out of the portal's frame
you shouldn't enter it, else you'll die in time.
Seeing yourself is proof of your survival,
Use that confidence as you tour into the portal.
Jesus Christ was a time-travelling creature,
and so was Aslan, from Chronicles of Narnia.
