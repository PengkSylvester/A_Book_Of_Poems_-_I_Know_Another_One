Dear customer, thank you for having the thought to reach out but,
The number you're trying to call is not reachable at the moment.
Please try again later, at a more appropriate or convenient time,
And we are sure that the number would \textit{then} join you on the line.
Unless, of course, the phone you're trying to call'll be switched off,
In which case, please contemplate that you've probably been blocked.
Anyway, thank you for using \xout{\phantom{okay?}}, we are going to hang up now.
We do not offer voicemails. Your call credit hasn't been affected. Ciao.
