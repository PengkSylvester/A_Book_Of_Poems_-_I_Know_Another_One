Stakeholder management is so annoying.
Two or more opposing directions all pulling.
I literally cannot aim to please everybody,
And negotiating for a middle-ground is so tricky.
But boy, does it feel good when things work out,
After the screams and shouts, a solution sprouts!
A settlement is reached which benefits everybody,
What was once contentious is now done happily.
