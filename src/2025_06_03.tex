\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{Compassion is to do the thing that cares for someone. It might be nice, it might be brutal. It might be for someone, it might be for yourself.}}


Love isn't ``do this for me so I can do that for you''

Love is to give you what you need, because I want to

Love isn't ``my way is the best so change yourself to fit me''

Love is to get your vote of confidence, voluntarily

Love doesn’t brag ``here's my good news in which I have much pride''

True love is patient and is kind. Love doesn't envy in its mind

Love isn't ``I'm upset at your boundaries, your guardrails''

Love is sharing, caring, daring to endure all things. Love never fails
