Nothing can ever sadden a man more than war
So much senseless destruction, all for what?
No child should have experienced such campaigns
To see yourself full of bullets, or up in flames?
Or worse, shell-shocked and rocked with guilt for surviving
``Why, God, did you not save Bill too?\ Or even little Ivan?''
Nothing could ever sadden me more than Nam in `66
Men begged for their mothers as I yelled for the medics
