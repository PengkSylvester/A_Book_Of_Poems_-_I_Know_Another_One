An open letter to Etta, the queen of stupidity.
I quit giving to you. I give up. That's just what's better.
I wish you your best. You were once someone I respected.
To whom much was given, much was also expected.
I'm not mad at you, you just disappointed me, kid.
If I were you, I'd ask why everyone's abandoning ship.
Your stupid desires to appear impressive have left me unimpressed.
I am not looking for your money, I am looking for people with common sense.
