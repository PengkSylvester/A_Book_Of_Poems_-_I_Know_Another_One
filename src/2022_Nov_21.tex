\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{The remains of the old must be decently laid away; the path of the new prepared. That is the difference between Revolution and Progress.} \newthought{- Henry Ford}}

Hiba mi vida, my wife with a silence of the lambs
Blink once if you're not enjoying my psalms.
I baked a loaf of bread this morning, flour and water
I'll be sharing it in an hour, with some wine and butter.
I'll bake another one for you, and feed a bite to you
I go do anything wey you want me to do.
I truly do love you babygirl buttercup
Even when I'm marking it from the bottom up.
