\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{A great deal of talent is lost to the world for want of a little courage}
\newthought{- Sydney Smith}}


``Money can't buy happiness'' they say, I guess,

Yet Happiness was a Nigerian girl we sold to the West.

``You will be addressed in the way you are dressed'',

Yet ``Don't judge a book by its cover'' is also stressed.

Make it make sense, that some say ``Dress to impress'',

While yet some say ``Nobody cares about your Rolex''

I say to you ``Be yourself, follow what's fun, do your best

To choose your path, and that'll make all the difference.''
