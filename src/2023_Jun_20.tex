\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{All wisdom warns. All advices are implicit warnings. Sometimes proverbs may seem to contradict themselves, but they are warning against different things.}}


Dear diary, I want to tell you my weird secret hobby.
It's a virtue, and just a few (two people) do know about it.
One of them is an old friend who's name is Sophie Greaves,
And the other is a close family member who I shan't reveal.
Too much of it will make me go blind – or so we've been told.
But have no fear, my dear daughter, I have it under control.
The term `weird’ is used conservatively in this here poem.
It's a much more charming hobby just for my free time.
