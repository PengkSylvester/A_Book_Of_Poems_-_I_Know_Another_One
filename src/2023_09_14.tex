\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{Sometimes a smiling "f**k you" can be a more honest "thanks" than a hug.}}

If one wades long enough into an abyss, the abyss wades into you,

Whisper “fuck you” to the darkness, and posess your \textit{nom de guerre}.

I would have no web of lies; give others no hope of becoming queen,  

Else my fiancée's umprompted flashbacks would turn random moments to cringe.

What if I didn’t want to kill \textit{John the Baptist}, even though I should?

It was too late now, I started the thing that was corrupt and stupid

And I must end it; else I turn into a monster who fosters room for rue.

So, surrounded by this present darkness, my North Star must remain true.
