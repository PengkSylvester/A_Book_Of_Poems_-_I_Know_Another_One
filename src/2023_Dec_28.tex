\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{If I think it's random, it's because I have no idea what's going on. And that's fine, there's no shame in not knowing stuff. I just shouldn't assume that just because the conversation makes no sense to me, it must not make sense at all.}}

Sometimes, someone will summarise me “Haha you're so random!”
But some things only seem random until you understand `em.
I'm not being random at all, each bit I do is a play on something,
Human aspirations, frustrations, hopes, fears, or suffering,
Or victories, spontaneous speeches, or investigative inquiries,
So call me Socrates; throwing verbal rocks at these mediocrities.
Am I an artist possessed to vent these in rhyming, rapper style?
Nay, I'm a playful philosopher freestyling for all of y'all.
