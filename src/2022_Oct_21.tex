\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{OPERATION MO.S.CO.W
Mo = Must do = Continue it now.
S = Should do = Schedule a date.
Co = Could do = Delegate it now.
W = Would do = Ignore it for now.}}

A rooster once crowed to wake him up in his secret life.
To a strange hotel, he stirred, he stared, and then he blinked twice.
Outside a window, a pharmacy sign blinked, one colour at a time.
There was no denial of where he had come into last night.
He'd been here for eight hours, time felt longer with this girl.
He had done the unforgivable - his stomach began to swirl.
What's his alibi?\ What in heaven's name does he tell his girlfriend?
I cried and tried to stand.\ That's when I woke up from the nightmare.
