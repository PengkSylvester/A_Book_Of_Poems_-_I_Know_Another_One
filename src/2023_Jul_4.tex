I have found myself to be somewhat of a thief.
Any normal person(s) may squirm at my belief.
So, hear me out... I consider myself a teacher of life's lessons.
I don't kill, steal, nor destroy; I take while no one's looking.
It's almost like the original owner wanted to give it away,
They left it there, unattended, unaware it would go M.I.A.
Sketches, maths sets, meat, calculators, books, gold dust.
Me, I keep my preciouses hidden; only showing what I must.
