When it's all said and done any line could be called a "pickup line".

It's just an icebreaker, statement, or comment to initiate a conversation.

If they're attracted, intelligent, and in a good mood, any line will work fine.

If they're not attracted to you, and in a bad mood, you'll get nothing.

Some of the best conversations start up by making observations;

You're both standing in a long queue, and say something like 

“Fake products are trending online, but your lotion smells original.”

It's now a shared experience because he heard, and knows you want to talk.

Or a “Are you Batch B?.. Your face isn't familiar.” keeps the talk small.
