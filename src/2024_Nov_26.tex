We are all passengers on planet earth
Zooming through space like a fly in a car
We have nowhere better to be, so we perch
Born into its free, fast ride to nowhere
Or maybe to somewhere, if the Gods favor us
Earth doesn't care much whether we're here
Understand there is no urge to preserve us to last
It has a pre-planned route with which to adhere
