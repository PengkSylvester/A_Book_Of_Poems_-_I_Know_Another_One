\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{When one of my trainees does something, and they deserve some kind of recognition for that, I must write them a recommendation for an award. If the award is written well, there's a much better chance that the award will actually be given. So my text is how my troops get judged so that they can be promoted. The importance of thinking clearly, and writing well is so high for my life; seniors and juniors alike, enjoy persons who have magic words.}}
