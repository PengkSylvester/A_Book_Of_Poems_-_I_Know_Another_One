\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{If your love is conditional, it is also transactional.}}

Dear Hibatullah, you are the most excellent of women
as I marry you, Hiba mi vida, I yearn for you, a strong yearning.
let our lips give testimonies of the glory of God's grace
let our hips give enpassioned splendor in love's holy embrace.
Please pray for me on the other side.\ Raise your head in pride
delighting on my appropriate behavior, and my robotic stride.
Dear future wife, I know our hearts will beat in rhythm, your spirit understands.
I remain your majestic and skillful servant; signed, future husband.
