\newthought{- Peter Obi}
I, Peter Obi, stand before you governors, ministers\resetlinenumber[1]
soldiers, revolutionaries, my brothers and sisters
There will be no tomorrow for me without your assistance 
I will ride stubborn into battle, and you know there will be blood
Today's war will firmly introduce them to my God
Ladies and Gents, this right here (lift your weapon)\ldots This is a sword.
You laugh, but it is not the killing machine, ye are warriors
Humans of valor, wallah valhalla calls to us!
