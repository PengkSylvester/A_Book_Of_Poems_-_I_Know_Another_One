I speak prophetically, in poetry.\ It's my specialty.
My words and messages manifest things for me.
Being made in the image and likeness of God,
I mean every word with so much power in this tongue.
We're more powerful than we can ever fully comprehend,
We are spirits giving life to clothes and raw food items,
So, I speak positively over our life, family, and situations,
to make well, tasty dishes play out of those for us.
