A woman can do anything a man can do,
it just takes her dedication, tried and true,
to overcome other people's preconceived prejudices,
and eschew her own self-doubt, to embrace life's many nuances.
For a poor family, the mother and father do random shit.
In an average family, one of them stays home to collect the profits.
For a rich family, they both work to provide together.
In a wealthy family, one makes the family business suffer.
