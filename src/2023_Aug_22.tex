\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{We like people best when they respond to us the fastest.}}

Two guys walk into a bar. 
\hspaced ``Hey donkey, get four beers'',
shouts one guy to the other.
\hspaced The bar woman overhears.
She said ``That's mean, what he says,
\hspaced I wouldn't let that happen to me'',
and the man replied, with the four beers
\hspaced ``It's OK, he aw\ldots he aw\ldots he always calls me donkey''.
