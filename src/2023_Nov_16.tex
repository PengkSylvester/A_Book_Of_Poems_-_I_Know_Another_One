I am a cursed man, and my accursedness is in my wants.
I am a slave, loyal to masters, not for benefits, but for faults,

Cowardice is not the word, these are just where my loyalty lies;
I need a Fossil watch, I need Uncle Emmanuel's approval,
I need my Marvelene, I need eight lines to be my identifier,
I need to give gifts to my juniors (X*10 dollars on their Xth birthday),
And lastly, I am cursed to always need to do things my way.
