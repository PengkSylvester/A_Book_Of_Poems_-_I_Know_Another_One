Interpretations of dreams are based on how the dreamer felt
literally, how you felt during the dream content.
The content itself is immaterial for prediction,
It tells us nothing about the present, past, or future.
If your spouse cheated in a dream; were you surprised or relieved?
What about dying in a dream; were you scared or fine to leave?
You killed in a dream; were you vengeful or left with no choice?
If you saved someone's life in a dream; was it a mistake or on purpose?
