\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{First they laugh at you. Then they fight you. Only afterwards do they leave you alone. But finally, they take the credit.}}


There are wild monkeys on site that scavenge through our rubbish

Over twenty of them, plenty, often enough for me to notice

Whenever one of them finds a juicy treat, carbs or a piece of meat

She quickly runs up a tree as fellows follow her to share from it

But she doesn't feel like sharing, and so yells at them to stop

And if they don't, she kicks them off. It's lonely at the top

If the follower fighter scavenger is much bigger than her, she suffers

Their struggle at the top will drop the food down for the others
