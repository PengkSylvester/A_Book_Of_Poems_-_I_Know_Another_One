\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{I have worked tirelessly to give you the ``for better'', but I also gave you this ``for worse''.} \newthought{- Micheal Irvin apologising to his wife.}}

She used to walk in rooms and look for her emergency door.
A fighter may surrender battles but a warrior never quits war.
She used to visualise and plan to fight for her escape route, 
but now\ldots she'll attack back, a war to protect the innocent few.
Now she's a warrior to feed and protect her people.
Great power, great responsibility to not feed her ego.
That warrior who fears hurting others, is the one revered.
The people's servant is loved, the people's tyrant is feared.
