Don't let your women talk about sex.
Don't let employees talk salaries.
When it kills the mystique for who's the best,
People promiscuously change companies.
Women want to know who is the better lay,
Do you intend to keep them against their wish?
Workers want to know who has the better pay,
Transparency will not make them \textit{not} fish.
