African parents used to beat their kids,
They used slaps as sentence punctuations.
The symbol `.' for full stop used to come as one slap.
So, the line ``We.\ Should.\ Not.\ Accept.\ Food.\ From.\ Strangers.'' was seven smacks.
This young generation has it the easiest
Parents now say ``period'' to indicate `end of sentence'
The unstoppable marching of time, I have learned,
is slowly guiding me to an inevitable unforeseen end.
