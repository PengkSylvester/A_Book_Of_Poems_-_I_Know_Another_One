\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{Self-discipline is salvation.}}

Title: \textit{What colour is your parachute?}\ A thesis on flirting.
In order to seduce, one needs confident conviction.
Any desire to escape is different from self-preservation \footnote{For whatever reason, when you don't get a positive reaction from your audience as you act out your bit, you may wish for the ground to open up and swallow you.}.
Do not overthink your stinks during your moments of shit eating. \footnote{Eating shit is the act of pretending to not notice your joke's failure, in an attempt to win back the audience because of your enthusiastic, confident, undeterred continued performance.}.
You are braver than you think, and capable of more than you imagine.
True preservation is to double down, put all your heart in.
Drown out the background, as you lift your voice and sing.
It doesn't matter how you found charisma, as long as it begins.
