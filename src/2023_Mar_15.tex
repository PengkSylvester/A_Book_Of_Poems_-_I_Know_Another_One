\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{Self-discipline is salvation.}}

Title: What colour is your parachute? A thesis on flirting.
In order to seduce, one needs confidence - that one thing.
Do not overthink charisma nor experiences of bombing \footnote{Bombing is the bad feeling of failing at your ongoing performance or joke, and still continuing it.}.
There's bound to be stinks during one's days of love-bombing \footnote{Love-bombing is the practice of showering a person with excessive, relentless, consistent affection and attention as a way of manipulating them to settle for a relationship.}.
But it's not yet the world's end, you should practice shit eating \footnote{Eating shit is the act of pretending to not notice your joke's failure, in an attempt to win back the audience because of your enthusiastic, confident, undeterred continued performance.}.
Any desire to escape is different from self-preservation \footnote{For whatever reason, when you don't get a positive reaction from your audience as you act out your bit, you may wish for the ground to open up and swallow you.}.
True preservation is to double down, put all your heart in.
Drown out the background, as you lift your voice and sing.
