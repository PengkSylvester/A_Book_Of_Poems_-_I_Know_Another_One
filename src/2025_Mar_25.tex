\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{Manipulation = Influence + Deception}}

I am constantly amazed by some things I have written,
And have almost never seen them coming from the beginning
When you write poetry, like I do, you impose strict rules on you
To deliberately make it harder to say what you think is true
To succinctly persuade the reader to see your point of view
And this is what makes my heart race; to see my unique pieces
My crystallised thoughts from certain times, perhaps places
Sometimes I agree with past me, sometimes marvel at the difference
