\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{Whatever chases a rat into the fire, must be hotter than the fire.}}


Dear diary, I met a friend yesterday, and she's the reason I'm starting this diary.
I plan to write to her every morning, or at least every day.
The girl's name is Hibatullah, and she has her battleaxe
up for the men who advance to her. 
I write to you, you read to her, so that we overwhelm her 
with consistency and our no-quit optimism, 
for a shift in her `\textit{paradigim}'.
So this is the first of my many reflections for my own sake and for her.
Love, peace out, yours faithfully, signed, \textit{et cetera}.
