Some people may not see the end of this year

Yet, take it for granted to have witnessed May

Every single week someone new says adieu

As the day comes to as end, look around you

Say goodbye to the sun as if for the last time

Because you may come to your end at 11:59

I pray death comes at God's own desired minute

And in the meantime, you make every second count
