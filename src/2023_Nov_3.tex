\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{Obstacles are those frightful things I see when I take my mind off my goals.}}

My Marvelene and I are new to intimacy, and \textit{relationshipping} in general,

She expects a lot of random acts taught to her gender, by the rom-com genre.

She got mad at me for not calling her on days when I didn't feel like it,

But forgot to mention that whenever I see a call from her, I always pick.

Whether I'm working, or chilling, or in the middle of Netflix and a meal,

I answer her calls in bid to improve how I guess she may feel.

And it's not enough, she wants calls, flowers, to be swept off her feet,

But I gift poems, and data, and my heart, in practical things.
