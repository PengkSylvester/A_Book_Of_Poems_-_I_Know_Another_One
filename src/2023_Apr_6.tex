\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{Do I want to have a permanently clean home, or do I want people living in it? Because I can't have both.}}

1.\ From the streets did she emerge;
and to the streets she did return
2.\ Because, I say unto you,
``She is for the streets''
3.\ So, be not weary when she must
return from whence she came
4.\ Hoes will hoe, so know fo sho;
the streets, are her home sweet home
\vspace{1em}
- The Gospel According To Future, Chapter 69, Verses 1-4
