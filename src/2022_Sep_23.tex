\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{Instead of doing what you have to do, do what you can do.}}

I'm not done with the leaders, we're the fulfillers of tomorrow.

Instead of doing what you must do, do what you can do.

Do less or do more than expectations, but do do it (``you said doodoo'').

Don't say ``f*** it'' to the suffering and then give up to the sorrow.

Expectations gives stress under the left breast, so stay true.

Make good choices, manage your wants, only suffer for needs.

It is an act of self-discipline to be kind to the things we can't control.

Be a good friend to yourself; celebrate your progress, however small.
