You can't imagine how homesick my heart truly feels.
I wish I could drive to my Marvelene at a dangerous speed,
She's my home and my neighbour, currently in Calabar,
She doesn't need to be troubled, for me to come save her,
With fires in my eyes, as I park at her parent's house,
I'll approach her and hug her with wide arms held close,
With as much ardour as can be present in a lover's embrace,
I wish to kiss my mistress on my return from absence.
