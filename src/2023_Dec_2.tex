The art that comes from deep within,
Does not need a commercial win.
Market values are confusing,
So, focus.\ Focus on the one in a million.
The muse; a movement; my thinking ink,
To get a certain way, to make a certain mark.
That’s what matters to me, not the public.
So, a toast; a toast to fuck off sale statistics.
