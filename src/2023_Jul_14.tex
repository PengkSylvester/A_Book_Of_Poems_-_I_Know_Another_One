\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{The dead outnumber the living 14 to 1, and we ignore the accumulated experience of such a huge majority of mankind at our peril.} \newthought{– Niall Ferguson}}

What is the secret purpose of my life and work?
When will I find out why I am who I was?
Will the realisation come milliseconds before my death?
Show me, please Lord, answer my calling, I beggeth,
So that I may bless the bitterness as well as the success.
Show me my purpose, Maker, so that I may do my best.
Lord, yes, I can sense you, I am learning it at last.
I am destined to make the way to the Legend of Jesus Christ.
