We bless, and we receive you, Maria, you beautiful mess.
In all of your uniqueness, strength and weakness. 
You are valuable.\ As God is with you, we wholeheartedly
be your sanctuary to keep you safe - your mother and me. 
Please understand that you are our everything and so much more.
You are a queen's daughter, there's things you shouldn't settle for. 
In your life, you should be seen, heard, connected to, 
befriended honestly, mutually supported, and respected too.
You're a king's daughter, words cannot express your might.
Don't have a job for job's sake nor endure not being liked.
