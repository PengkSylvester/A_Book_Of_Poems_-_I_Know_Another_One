The curse of capability

smart capable entrepreneurs tend to put themselves in situations to do complex things because they're capable of doing
those things.

Keep it simple, stupid.

The hardworking smart entrepreneurs have a complex web of businesses.

Remove things.

The two most important questions for your employees and customers are
``What would be the one thing that if I took away that you would not want to work here anymore?''
``What would be the one thing that you wish I added that would make you stay forever?''

They'll tell you what to eliminate and what to keep.
