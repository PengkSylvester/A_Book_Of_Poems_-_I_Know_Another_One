\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{Spaced repetition helps me to reinforce learning.}}

Dear diary, the funniest thing ever, happened today at sea.
A British vessel had the balls to reach out for help, to me.
It was almost like they had forgotten the year was Nineteen-Forty-Five,
And were hoping that an ally or neutral party would keep them alive.
Oh, how I hate those damn, posh, infidel English men and women.
``Mayday, mayday, this is Her Majesty's Ship 9A, and we're sinking.''
As if I would give a heaping hot damn if the enemy were to drown.
So, I said ``Zis is German submarine.\ Vat are you sinking about?''
