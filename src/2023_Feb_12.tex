\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{A picture is a poem without words.} \newthought{- Quintus Horatius Flaccus}}

It's our two-month anniversary, two years to go.
By this time in twenty-twenty-five, time won't be slow.
The planning days will melt together after invites are sent.
\textit{``You're cordially invited to witness Marvelene wed Pengk.''}
We will do it right, will not die, will have 124 days left.
You'll make a white attire; I'll make a white breast pocket. 
I love you, Marvelene, my mingled \textit{pringle}, never forget.
I am enjoying us very much; we are a perfect match with no end.
