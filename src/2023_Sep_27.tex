\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{Life is a gift. That's why it is called `present'. But it is not yours, it is a gift to those around you.}}

Cells die and get replaced with younger cells, to better preserve health.
People die and get replaced with protégés, to better preserve wealth.
Companies die too; when competitors provide a better service.
Industries die soon; when the favourable legislation changes.
A legislative arm of government may die due to dirty bribes.
A country's monetary system may die due to unproductive lives.
Before the nation dies, it gets absorbed by a colonial power
And they fill cells with younger cellmates, for free labor.
