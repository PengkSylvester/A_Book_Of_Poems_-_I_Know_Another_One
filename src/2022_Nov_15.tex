I sit and stir betwixt a pair of trees with spits of golden hair,
I see some birds clothed with living colors of brown and red,
They flit and dart, they tweet and fart, the sweet beat 
soft tune of their sounds keep me in my hypnotized state.
They seem to sing, “Come fly with us.\ You have a feather”
I wish I could tell them it's for quilling my mating ritual.
I wish I didn't have to rhyme every time I spoke,
