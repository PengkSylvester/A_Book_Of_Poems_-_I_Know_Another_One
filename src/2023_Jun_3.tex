\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{I hope that someday someone else might choose to be adopted by me.}}

I would argue that few poets probably pour pain out their soul 
like I do. Many waste ink in absurd humdrum issues
This type of rhyming; each line an intricate bar, 
that’s not done easily with the snap of a finger - 
that’s from thinking, writing on a daily basis, 
constantly jotting down poetic lines and phrases.
It's difficult to both rhyme and get my point across 
That’s why people like me do be getting mad props!
