\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{When you marry a person, you marry their family too.} \newthought{- Kevin Kelly}}

Well, what do people have when things are insane;
when they're truly lost, when they're absurdly rich but in pain? 
Well, they have the structure around them to induce
the sense of security their sacrifices did produce.
They have their partner; their wife or husband
They have their children, they have their friends,
they have their parents, and their siblings and communal brethren, 
they have their God and social structure around them.
When they need to fall prey to suffer unfortunate cruelty
The helpers that Satan needs to kill lets you know theirs truly.
