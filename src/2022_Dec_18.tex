\newthought{- Footballer}
I remember the first time I saw a football;
leathery pig skin of pentagons, it was all
too heavy, too grand.\ I didn't understand
the destiny, my career to play it with my gang.
I'll try my best, my everything, to hold us together,
I will move as one, find the openings, and be stronger.
Look at us now, Mama, we're at the World Cup.
I am Pele, Messi, Mbappé.\ Let's see if I f*ck up.
