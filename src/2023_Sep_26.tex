\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{Death is inevitable. Someday, I too will be unresponsive and pale.}}

Death will come to me when I run out of will or resources. 
Ready or not, I go from fetus till rot, no matter my genus.
Even no matter my genius, organ transplants cannot save me.
Horrific mortality must just catch up with me eventually.
Picking up my daughter with one arm, I thought of this.
Everyone alive will someday rest in peace; deceased.
Her own face will one day be limp; what used to be animated.
She'll be dead, and look like it; drained out, emptied.
