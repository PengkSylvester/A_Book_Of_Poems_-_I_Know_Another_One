I don't come up with these poems by mere thinking.
The words just sort of, come to me, like a marvelous thing.
It's a very active process - I find the words by feel.
I touch and find a hate for neckties? Then, pen down that spiel.
Hell, I write a pentuple entendre, where three are risqué.
My rhetorics feed off of its hips, nips, yips, and I mix it my way.
I hold back for those in the dark, else it's written with sextuple meanings, 
I sit and taste my desire for more lips, H, N, \& Y. Honey, it's giving.
