``People, poets who name their poems are so weird'',
says this guy, me, who numbers his poems instead.
Titles usually don't add anything to an amateur's piece,
But they write it there anyway because of the blank space.
It is a clause that may take away from your poem's impetus,
so I leave it silent, secret; I leave it as a pregnant pause,
I am not so desperate to summarise the poems I file
With superfluous titles like Cinderella, or Raw, or Philophile.
