\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{We sing to commit to memory the words of our elders.}}

Dear diary, she's probably in the air right now, high as a kite
I pray she's alright.\ I like what she does to me.\ Focus is hot.
Time will make me tell who she is and who she's not. 
She's defensively chill, intellectually hot.
Temporarily illiquid, from travelling back and forth. 
She's beholding.\ She's sexy.\ What are my intentions?\ I forgot.
Oh yeah, to get her.\ To scheme her into choosing me as her partner.
Until then, Love, peace, signed, \textit{et cetera}.
