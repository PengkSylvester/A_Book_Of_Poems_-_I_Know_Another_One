\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{We covered so much distance without realising it...}}

Why do black men wear neckties?
It serves no purpose in my eyes.
It doesn't give warmth like a scarf.
It doesn't fasten my shirt like a button.
Some white man once wore it to stand out,
and now, years later, it's the standard?
It's an unnatural unfunctional addition to me; 
a fashion accessory with no history.
