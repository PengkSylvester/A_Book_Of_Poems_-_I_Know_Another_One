Sometimes, I think thoughts that I don't want others to view.
And no, they are not evil thoughts, just temporary truth.
Why should I tell you how I'm doing, when I'll be better tomorrow?
Why should I tell you anything at all that you don't need to know?
I share them anyway, because I want to be seen by you.
My annoying life would be more annoying if not viewed.
Maybe that was a reason humanity invented God,
To have someone to show the thoughts that no one else got.
