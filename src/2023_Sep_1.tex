\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{To me, the sweetest words in the English language are, ''Granddad, would you like to ...?".} \newthought{- Ron Leslie}}

\vspace{-1.4cm} \textcolor{gray}{\rule[2.5mm]{7.4in}{0.4pt}}
\vspace{0.85cm}

Gather under my mango tree, my imaginary, unborn, adult children. 

Wouldn’t it be nice to reminisce on how it is I wooed your Mum;

Man, it wasn’t a case where `I just smiled, and she had fell apart'.

I had to work to win your mother’s heart.\ Your mother’s smart.

I stood out from the many as the one who was the perfect sir;

With the right chemistry, history, charisma, daily literature,

Et cetera, and yes, you heard me, I wrote to her every day;

Your Dad was a focused man with a faithful plan, and good pay.
