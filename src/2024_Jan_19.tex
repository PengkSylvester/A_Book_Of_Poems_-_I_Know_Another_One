This whiny bitch, this controlling complainer, this pesky puppy!
This jealous dog wants my constant presence, or else has anxiety!
I can walk her without a leash because she stays, which is endearing,
But she doesn't let anybody else, even Mydear, take care of her, which is annoying!
She's an adopted orphan, and as her guardian I feel responsible for her well-being,
But I don't have any personal privacy any more, and it's maddening!
She doesn't make me smile anymore, but I keep watching her;
For the loyalty I get, and for being the only one who understands her.
