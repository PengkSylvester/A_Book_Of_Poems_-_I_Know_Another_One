\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{Everyone has the right to freedom of opinion and expression; this right includes freedom to hold opinions without interference and to seek, receive and impart information and ideas through any media and regardless of frontiers.}}

When I, an opinion-haver, bring forward an idea
One that you steal outrightly, that doesn't cause me despair.
My opinions, beliefs, and experiences are not me.
The sound of my voice, and the gait of my walk are not me.
Neither are my thoughts, nor my thoughts about my thoughts.
They are tools.\ I am the user.\ I want to find the magic words
Ones that strike me as right and true, and are then stress-tested
To see which collection of magic words stand as the best sentences.
