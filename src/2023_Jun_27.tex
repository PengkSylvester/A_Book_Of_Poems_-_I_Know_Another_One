An AI already exists to write great poems, Jesus!
But, I am \textbf{not} using ChatGPT to write any of my pieces.
It doesn't take a genius to know that that would be pointless.
Like using AI helpers for winning every game of chess.
I intend not to ghost-write or plagiarise my book of poems,
But rather to create the brain that can create intricate rhymes.
So, if I'm ever locked up in a battle with just a pen and pad,
I won't need to cheat, I have the brain to improvise a rap.
