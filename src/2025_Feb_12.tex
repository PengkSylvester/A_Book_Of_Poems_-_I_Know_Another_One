Dear Valentine, it's about time I give you flowers either as beautiful as you are
Or as sweet smelling as \textcolor{blue}{\href{https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Clitoria_fragrans?useskin=vector}{clitoria fragrans}}, which looks like your pusswah,
The birth flowers for december are holly and paperwhite daffodils,
And that's how you are, my darling, compared to thorny other women,
I delight to sniff your petals, and your nectar is sweet to my taste,
You'll keep blossoming and blossoming in season, turning dew to droplets,
How flagrant you are, my darling!\ Your perfume is more than any spice,
You are a vineyard, a garden, a bouquet, and a flower.\ Happy Valentine's
