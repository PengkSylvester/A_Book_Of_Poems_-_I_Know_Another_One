Dear diary. Dear Maria, I had not seen you in a while
Then I saw you nearby today, with Marvelene and I
You watched your Mum and Dad kiss for a first (and long) time
Whenever your mum melted, I stopped and said ``I've got you''
Boo, when you overflow as our baby, know I've got you too;
Whether it's tears, giggles, vomit, sleep, pee, emo, or poo,
I've got you, I'll be sure to change you. No pun intended.
Until then, all you get are the rhymes, and Mum and Dad on the bed.
