At the moment of dread, I don't feel it in the head, but in the chest
Not directly where the heart is, more like an ache in the breasts

I can't think clearly as my ears blush and I feel a blood-rush in my lungs
Like a tummyache in my upper torso, a sudden stiffness to my tongue

And it's not within my control to turn it down or even off
Eating food or drinking water at that moment makes it worse

That's when my fight or flight response kicks itself in to save the day
I walk away or yell ``how dare you?'' or go closer to investigate
