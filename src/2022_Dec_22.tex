It feels good to know for sure - to feel and say ``I get it now!''
It makes me confident, to lean in a positive valence of ``Eureka!''
I'm not usually in opposite of confident; swimming in anxiety.
My gut either strongly agrees or disagrees before my first sip.
I don't enjoy the test-and-see uncertainty of \textit{that, or this, or those, or this}
When I get certain, I've dispensed with the energy for `excess entropy'.
It feels good to fully focus on the play I have, not `what could be'.
I can give the benefit of the doubt, until proven to be contrary.
