Let us pray,
Dear God, we humbly acknowledge that you do for us what we could never do on our own. %
Your inestimable grace granted us life decades ago, and we are grateful we are not gone. %
We are gathered here today to mourn our irreplaceable sister, your dear daughter, %
who passed away on the 30th of March.\ I fondly remember her, and sorely miss her. %
Life isn't fair sometimes; we make some plans only for things to end up not how we planned it. %
Ngozi Ukwandi was my Aunty, lovely and lively, and is now limp, unresponsive, drained out, emptied. %
Help us who remain to comfort one another as we tell stories of her life every now and then. %
May she painlessly rest in your peace, Lord, as we commit Ngo's spirit into your hands.
We pray this through Christ our Lord.\ Amen.
