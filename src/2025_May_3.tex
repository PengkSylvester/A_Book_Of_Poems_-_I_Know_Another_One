\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{}}

Shortly after writing this letter, I am going to kill myself.

This is not a cry for aid, any help will be too little, too late.

I naturally thought of life as getting better, and not worse.

It was my illusion that hard work and discipline always pays off.

It's a common hope, and it's that faith that keeps people going.

But it may be false in my case, so I give up, I throw the towel in.

The universe is indifferent to any survival of the fittest.

It punishes its children for being the best and brightest.

I think I've had enough, it's necessary to end my anguish.

My brain death is a consummation devoutly to be wished.

I wrote this suicide note in a daze, a morbid excitement.

Despite all my work, this decision is the card I've been dealt.

I want to cry, I don't know why.\ Goodbye world, hello death.
