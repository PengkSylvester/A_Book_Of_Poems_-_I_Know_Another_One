\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{Perspicacity. The quality of having a strong insight into things.
Perspicacity. A clarity of vision, a penetrating discernment.}}


When writing, look at the real thing you are trying to describe


Spend the most brainpower in observing the muse in your mind


Than in looking at your words, articulation, judgement, rhyme


This is also true for drawing, painting, sculpting, mime


Keep your mind's eyes on it, realise it opens up to let you inside


Do not be tricked away to self-consciousness at this time


While creating, your attempt is not where you should keep your eyes


You are not the subject, your try is the reaction to the perfect line


When you're done, then sure, you can touch it up, edit it, refine.
