I find myself re-realising a few things I used to know

Like a meditation's return to breath, after the mind's roam

I'm relearning to give, with no expectations of a return

I lost my way before, and so I'm sorry for what I'd done

I did something for you, and thought ``now do something for me''

But that's not love. A better system is to give things for free

I didn't sift thoughts as they came, the fame had gotten to my head

So I'm sorry for gifting what I should have held back instead
