\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{Impostor syndrome is the fear of being exposed as being less talented than people think one is, often because opportunity is owed to social cumulative advantage rather than actual talent or skill.}}

The past is like a certainty a fixed distance away. 
The future is a sea of good and bad potentials every day.
As entropy flows forward like a river of no return,
We are babies in baskets, pushed away after being born.
My death would be a sinking, an end to future potentials,
A nothingness, I'm thinking, but my best guess is still a guess.
Will my antlantic sink be permanent, or just a `ciao for now'?
Anyhow, that is the question; what and what not does it allow?
