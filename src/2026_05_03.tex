The 10/10/Forever rule

Who are the 10 people that you would invest in for the next 10 years, who'd pay you forever?
Wealth creation is a Who strategy, not a How strategy.

Remember the rich man who gave talents to his three servants.

If you have money, find yourself some good servants to give it to.

If you don't have money, find yourself a rich master to make even wealthier.

Don't consume what is given to you, multiply it.
