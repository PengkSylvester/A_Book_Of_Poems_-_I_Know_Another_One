\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{It’s the life in my years, not the years in my life, that counts.}}


\textit{``Dear reader, I hope this letter finds you feeling supreme.

I seek to acquaint you of my affection and esteem for Marvelene.

She's the most excellent of women, and we are yearning to get wed.

I'll play my part, ready to war thousands for the object of my world.

If I have proved myself to you, worthy of that honour, I pray,

To receive your blessings as you attend our wedding day.

Please pray for us on the other side, raise your head in pride,

Delighting on our appropriate behavior, and majestic stride.''}
