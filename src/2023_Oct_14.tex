Is collecting literal flags that are red,
as a sort of super niche hobby, a red flag?
I'm having a date right now, for my sweet tooth,
And thought, I wish my wife had a collection too.
Alas, no.\ Her hobby is way more queer.
She is obviously a fan of fags, it's clear.
I hate smoking, but she has a gay merry time with it.
Our only mutual capital interest is in semantic drift.
