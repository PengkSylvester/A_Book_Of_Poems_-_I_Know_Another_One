He was in ecstasy, a huge smile on his face, 

as his wife moved forwards, then backwards, 

forward, then backwards again... back and forth,

in and out, in and out, she was feeling the sweat 

trickling down the small of her back, she was 

getting near to the end, her heart was furious,

her face was flushed, and she moaned, softly at first, 

then louder, and, finally exhausted, she sighed and let 

her husband have what was coming for him since

``I can't park the damned car! You do it! You smug prince!"
