Growing up, I was drawn to adventure and grandeur,
I dreamed of a life filled with excitement extraordinaire.
I soon grew out of it, enough to see that was impossible.
But, instead of viewing this life as dull or unremarkable,
I romanticise the mundane, but it ain't a cope to survive,
I have become aware of the blissful things before my very eyes.
It's a genuine recognition of my rich everyday moments.
There is beauty to be found if only I'm looking to see it\ldots

There is beauty to be found if only I'm looking to be it.
