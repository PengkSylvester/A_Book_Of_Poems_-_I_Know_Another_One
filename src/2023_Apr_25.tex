Dearest Marvelene, I'm sure you'll permit me to hand-write my reply
You'll soon see a breakthrough in regard to seeing your loyal guy
The radio is playing ``Black is Beautiful'' by Chronixx, and I can't help but feel
Rightfully moved and sickened by the needless killing of a human being
It should never have happened, and you'll get our voices heard by speaking soberly
Of course I'll protest too, I am glad people care enough to gather in solidarity
Accept my thanks for all you do, your wholesome play, and brilliant work
I've been blessed enough to have you, your intelligence, your wisdom, and your love.
