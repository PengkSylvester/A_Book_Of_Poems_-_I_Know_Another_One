\textit{[A CAT]}
\resetlinenumber[1]
What are you eating?\ Give me some from the heap.
I don't know yet how you make it taste so sublime, you witch!
Scratch my back, and in return, I'll give you the privilege.
It's all about me me me - ow!\ Don't spray me with that water.
I don't need you, I'll go hunt and fight for supper.
Love me tender, my purr is yours.
Will you miss me though?\ Hm?
Should I bring you some of my kill?
\textit{I feline}\ldots I feel I'm special, and you know that's a fact.
\textit{This has been a poem from the perspective of a cat.}
