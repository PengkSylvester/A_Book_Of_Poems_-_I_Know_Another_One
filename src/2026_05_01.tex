Your Mother and Father chose to fulfil their royal roles of birthing a Lady/Lord. 

They called on you to ascend out of the darkness of nonbeing's void

And into the infinite beauty of this divine present as an extension of God

Made in His image and bestowed with blessings to bring gifts to the world.

But I don't know you. Maybe you were a mistake, created in one lustful night 

By two ignorant teenagers, who then fearfully nurtured you into being ripe. 

Maybe they regret it and wish they abandoned you because they were unprepared enough

But their family/religion had forced them to add a new victim to this thing called life.
