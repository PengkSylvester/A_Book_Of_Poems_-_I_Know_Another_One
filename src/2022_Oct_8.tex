\newthought{- Hiba}
I hate your oh-so-deep sponsored-by-Shakespeare poem.\resetlinenumber[1]

I know ``to be or not to be'' is true, but okay, what then?

Dear poet, I do not know what you are on and on about.

You make unnecessary references and sit back with no doubt

that you are making sense as our self-appointed poem deity.

But if we ``meh'' your poetry, you are not communicating.

It's your job to go Picasso your words onto my window.

Lose the depth, make some meaning, be more memorable.
