\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{Love is beautiful when shared, and perfect when given.}}

I could never forgive myself for killing the not-so-innocent girl, 

I had done the unforgivable - my stomach would always swirl.

I broke myself deeper into fears, tears for doing the dismal chore.

I had killed \textit{John the Baptist}, to prevent \textit{her} delusions of grandeur.

It was how the story had to go, for peace to reign for all of us.

And I had done it for Christ's sake, for Marv's sake, My `Jesus'.

I spent some time trying doggedly, ruggedly stumbling, fumbling forward,

And Marv later forgave me with open arms for the marriage I offered.
