\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{Everything's a poem if you let it be.}}


I used to have amped up energy in my youth

Such bright speed but I was starved of direction

My attitude kept oscillating between good and bad

I remember that as a kid I rewired my room

My parents were shocked when they found out

They grounded me for a week

Until I could conduct myself properly

I objected, but my resistance was shut down
