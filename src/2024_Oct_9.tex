\hypertarget{\the\value{daynumber}}

\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{}}


I was born twenty-four years ago, without my consent.
I was forced to roleplay a Christian child by my parents.
I studied books and passed tests, without it fully making sense.
I just looked forward (in past tense) to leaving my adolescence.
Now, I'm an adult, and nothing is quite - how do I say - correct.
For instance, everyone wants me to start buying pampers.
And, my partner Pengk wants me to, instead, start a business.
So much risk, but I grew up to have fun - to enjoy - not to have stress.
