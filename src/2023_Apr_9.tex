\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{God's not dead}}

The recovery of a lost one is not an easy feat
To seek, undeterred, even though things are looking bleak
Trying doggedly, ruggedly stumbling, fumbling forward
Typical of the hopeful to resurrect the mourned
That reunion of the parties, finder and \textit{findee}
There is no sweeter feeling, no `more delicious' meal
Though there was doubt, I never lost my faith
Throughout my searching, all I ever did was prayed
