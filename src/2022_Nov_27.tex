\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{Inhale suffering (yours and others), exhale compassion.}}

I'm a husband with no ring.
Husband material; a stud and a best friend.
I am a well-learned king.
Looking forward to the kingdom I will bring.
When I re-discovered you, it was as if I had peeked
at a universal plan - a compass, so to speak.
I am no longer a slave to the sands of time.
I can reliably see what comes next in line.
