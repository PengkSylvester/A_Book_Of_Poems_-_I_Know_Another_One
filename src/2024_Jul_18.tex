Dear diary, it is now Day 14 of 16 in this American prison.
My parents forced me to come visit here for no obvious reason.
They think it's beautiful and rich, but it's ugly as sin,
And ironically, ``Land of the Free'' is what it said on the tin.
But I'm not free from them, to fly to where I want to be,
They seized my life and liberty, and pursued this happiness for me.
But this is not the bride I want, the USA is not my destiny.
I'm only here because they promised to fly me to TZ.
