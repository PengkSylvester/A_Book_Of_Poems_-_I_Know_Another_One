Every now and then it's nice to put down your phone;
Go outside and look at the clouds and cars, all alone;
Actually touch some grass; pick up your head and breathe;
Reacquaint yourself with Nature; realise it lacks what you need;
Discover the changes in your world, and the strangers unconcerned;
Sit down and think, you did all of this in ten minutes and
Take a hard pass and immediately go right back to scrolling.
Balance is important.\ It keeps the fun from getting boring.
