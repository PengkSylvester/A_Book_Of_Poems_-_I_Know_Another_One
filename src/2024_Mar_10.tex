Genesis 40; A cup-bearer had a dream where he was with the Pharaoh's cup,
and his reaction was to serve good wine, and diligently do his job.
He learns the dream means he will be restored to his former position.
A baker confesses his own dream after that favorable interpretation;
He'd dreamt he carried the Ruler's basket full of baked goods
and had watched as wild birds had ate up all the foods.
The baker wasn't diligent with protecting the Pharaoh's things,
and that resentful attitude had followed him into his dreams.
Anyway, the baker discovers the dream means Pharaoh will hang him in days,
And the prediction came true.\ The man was hung, and wild birds ate his face.
I think the important part of a dream is in reacting at the moment.
My knee-jerk reactions to my unrequited situations tell me about my depths.
