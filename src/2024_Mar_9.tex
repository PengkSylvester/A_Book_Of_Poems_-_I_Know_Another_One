    \textit{[TO MY FATHER]}
    \vspace{0.4cm}

    Onowu sir, I pray you're having a good day.
    \vspace{0.4cm}
    My apologies for being unable to write to you yesterday, I do know you always take great pleasure in hearing of my
    welfare.\ I have been developing my competence on the Dam Project subject matter beforehand, so that I can conduct
    myself to properly execute the social and technical aspects of planting this battery for our use.
    \vspace{0.4cm}
    Your property in Utè is a good example of a place that can benefit from reliable 24/7 power supplied to it, but please
    safeguard your expectations until we've been given the go-ahead.\ It feels incredible to know the predictably-good
    advantage we could accrue to ourselves with this positioning.
    \vspace{0.4cm}
    My voluntary adoption of the responsibilities is another thing that has been on my mind, as opposed to them having
    been thrust upon me.
    \vspace{0.4cm}
    It's a privilege and an honour to me that you'll potentially invest capital into our project, to be transparently managed
    by my team, and I promise to guard it with my life.\ Protecting someone else's money is not something to be joked with,
    especially when I know it would take me years to replace it.
    \vspace{0.4cm}
    I hope you are enjoying the perspicacity of my remarks, and I will write to you again soon.
    \vspace{0.4cm}
    I have not much else to say besides goodnight; and remain
    \vspace{0.4cm}
    Your dutiful humble Son.

\end{fullwidth}
