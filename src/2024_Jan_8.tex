I got a puppy today.\ A female American Eskimo.
She's madly in love with me, and follows me everywhere I go.
This\ldots pet is mine.\ There are others like her, but this one is mine.
And I'll continue to play my part as her best friend,
until death does its part against my wishes.
The bitch is currently too scared to jump down to lower staircase,
but bravely does it to catch up with me, her bestie.
She's an intelligent little pudding, and probably will survive without me.
