\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{Truth sits upon the lips of the dying. Sad that people have to be on their deathbead to work this life stuff out.}}

The renown of riches or beauty is fleeting and frail,
Mental excellence is the more splendid lasting detail.
Come make capital in this capital by teaming with your teeming guest.
I'll be your angelic engine empowered for this heroic quest
Of creating a better road with un-tolled access for the poor
A public pool in Brick City is the answer to it all.
I will knock on every door and raise the funds for the road
By getting pool memberships and using the profits for some good.
