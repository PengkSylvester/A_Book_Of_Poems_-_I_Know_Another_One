The Abuja Electricity Distribution Company's supply is not constant.
Their kilowatt production is less than their population's demand.
So, to serve everyone in a Y number of districts,
Only an X number of districts will actually get electrics
At any given time (where the number Y is greater than X).
This is called load-shedding, and to solve it needs investments.
I have a complex solution, since our government has no funds;
Supply a constant metred power to people under certain tax brackets.
