\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{Depressed people have an accurate view of what they want, but are unaware of how to let go of it.}}


When I've had enough of everything I've ever wanted


The only feeling left to be felt is that of being dead


When I've tasted fine wines, and had my fair share of wealth


There remains nothing else to do, but that sleep that's ultimate


To put an end to the ghouls and ghosts that laugh and scream in my head


Is a desire to be wished, but I remain alive instead


Jesus, help me find a new experience or extravagance


That can be fun enough to become my life purpose
