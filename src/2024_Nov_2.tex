I use money to get what I want, like everyone does.
It's a social, lubricational way to keep people's records.
It's just that, money by itself doesn't make more money.
Money \textit{buys} things that can make more money.
There are only two ways to become a money-maker;
In exchange for valuables, or performed skilled labour.
A `valuable' is any beautiful thing I am willing to buy;
A stock, currency, art piece, property, or a kidnapped guy.
A `skilled labour' is anything I can't or won't do for myself;
Magic, therapy, banking, poetry, or surgery (organ harvest).
