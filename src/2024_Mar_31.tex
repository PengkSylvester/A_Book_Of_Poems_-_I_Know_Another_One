\textit{[TO JESUS]}
If only I could love you as you loved me,
I want to dedicate my life to you.
If only I could die for you as you died for me,
I want to be zealous for you, Lord.
You always give more than you take from me,
You lived and died for me;
My challenge is to aim high, or die trying.
`History has got its eyes on me'.
You came up gladly,
Freely given to us all.
An infant in an instant,
A carpenter's son.
Suffice it to say,
Your sacrifice paid
when you whispered `it is done'.
My judge don't be far from me.
Come quickly, \textit{Was To Come And Is To Come}.
