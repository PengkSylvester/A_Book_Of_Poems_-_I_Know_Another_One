My love, the one promised to me, promiscuity is not what I do,
In this land, every beauty I see are only reflections of you,
And there's no flirt that can compare to your affection
With all your heart.\ I love you beyond the limits of imagination,
Only you can please me, and so I yearn for a conference.
I pray that my absence has not ignited for your former indifference.
I miss you so much, and I just can't wait to come and see.
Your love for me is home mid-sea, oh divinely gorgeous ebony.
