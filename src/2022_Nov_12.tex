\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{At some point you will have to stop running and take a stand.}}

The more options one considers, the more buyer’s regret.
Is it best to get the first choice from a list but end up less 
satisfied, or go with your gut for a good enough outcome and be glad? 
It'd take you three years, anyway, before you'd know good or bad.
I'm picky with restaurants and movies.\ What are you picky with?
Set an external standard that if it doesn't meet then cut it.
Look at the pricing, reviews, deadlines of when to choose.
Then go with your gut on the available, and see how it goes.
