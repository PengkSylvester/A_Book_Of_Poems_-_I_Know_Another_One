A hard-worker's reward is more work.\ Am I burned out?
Rushing poems is bad, and I know my last one went south.
But with a pen in hand and my sharp mouth, I will die doing what I love.
Find what you love and let it drain you is my cure for burnout.
When I'm exhausted of the monotony, 
ineffective on expected productivity, 
or catch myself saying ``I can't take much more of this shit'', 
I just accept my determined death in the relationship.
