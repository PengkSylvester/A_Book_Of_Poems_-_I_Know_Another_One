When a child is born, the offspring needs its mother.
But to a teenager, the mother's care may be a bother,
Life goes on a bit further, and brings a balance to the order.
In adulthood, the mother and daughter give each other,
Until the parent starts to resent the kid for taking over.
Because most beginner `needers' do not want to be the needer,
They still yearn to be the giver; Mother wants Daughter to owe her.
And later, Mother dies, Daughter cries, and pays debt to newborn leader.
