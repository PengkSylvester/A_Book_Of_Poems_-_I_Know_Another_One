\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{To me, dreams don't predict the future. They show me how I would feel about a situation.
Someone may dream of their cheating spouse and feel angry. Why the anger instead of being not surprised?
A baker may dream of their Pharoah's bread coming to harm and feel nothing. Why the passive indifference instead of a desire to protect the Pharoah's bread?
A Pharoah may dream of cannibalisms and feel a desire to help. Why the desire to help them instead of feeling hateful disgust?
I'm sure you get the point. The conviction they all feel shows them their own character.}}
