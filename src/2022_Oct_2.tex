\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{Never give up on a dream just because of the time it will take to accomplish it. The time will pass anyway.} \newthought{- Earl Nightingale}}

If you study the greeks properly,

you learn to love the word beauty.

Don't delude that beauty is close to Godliness.\ It is wrong.

For example, goodness takes the necess-ity of song

and infuses the ultimacy of truth as a reminder.

I sing to find / recollect the words of our elders.

Beauty isn't goodness, so don't be a fool.

``Beauty is the perfect use of a good tool''
