\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{Aflattus is a Greek word meaning the impulse of creative power or inspiration, especially in poetry, considered to be of divine origin.}}

Sometimes, after I sense an intense experience, I hear a bell. 
When I hear a bell, I write its poem.
When I write a poem I consolidate my thoughts.
Before my thoughts are consolidated, I've fought and fought,
to find the words that ring truest to my subject.
``How long does each take, Sir Slick-with-the-pen?''
It depends\ldots Y'know it's not exactly a science.
