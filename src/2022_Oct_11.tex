Dear diary, she's like a stallion who'd let me ride her.
But would she ride her fastest if I yelled out ``Hiba!''?
I'll reassure her there's no other.\ Actions speak over lip.
Lead her to water every day, although I can't make her sip.
She's accepted our relationship, and I will still abide
I'm ride-or-die as long as I'm alive, hungry, never satisfied.
She's my life. 
I cannot flatter myself that I found Hiba because I was a `good picker'.
Hiba rings a bell in me for canoe partner.
I am hers faithfully.\ Love, etc.
