\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{``Well, I’m not saying I love you unconditionally, it’s just, we’ve been through a sh*tload of conditions, and I’m still here.''} \newthought{- Marty Byrde, to his wife}}

I'm not (and will never be) rewarded enough for my work. 
I ask myself ``I'm getting paid for this?!\ the money's too much''
See the paralysing paradox, Doc?
Payment, recognition, results, rewards don't matter. 
They just delay my rage, my death, my here-after.
I don't look forward to the reciprocating gifts of the gods,
I look forward to dying while pushing the love 
of my life up that hill past the point of tiredness.\ I'm Cicyphus.
