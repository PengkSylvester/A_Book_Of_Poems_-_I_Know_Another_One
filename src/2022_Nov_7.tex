\marginnote[1mm]{\textit{Daily activities that healthy people tend not to think about take tolls on people with chronic illness. We start the day with a certain number of spoons, and play our day around not running out. Socializing might cost spoons, using the stairs might cost spoons, lifting things might cost spoons, etc. For example, Skylar might say ``It's a low spoons day today, I don't think I'll go out.''} \newthought{– Christine Miserandino}}
